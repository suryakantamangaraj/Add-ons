Privacy Policy for Code Snap

Data Collection

Code Snap does not collect any personal information. The extension only processes the code snippets you choose to capture.

Data Usage

Code snippets are processed locally on your device. No data is sent to our servers or third parties.

Image Storage

Screenshots are saved locally to your device. You have full control over where and how to share them.

User Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no user data to manage. All processing happens locally on your device.

Updates to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Users will be notified of any significant changes.
